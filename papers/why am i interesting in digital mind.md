# Why Am I Interested in Digital Mind ?

I am fascinated by one question:

**How does a mind emerge from a system?**

My interest began with human cognition. While reading novels, I often experience vivid internal worlds filled with places, characters, memories, and histories. 
This led me to wonder how the brain constructs and maintains such complex world models.

**As I explored Artificial Intelligence and Cognitive Science, I discovered that many of the questions I care about are shared by both fields:**
- How is a world model formed?
- How does a persistent self emerge?
- Can artificial agents develop minds of their own?

**This repository is my research journal, where I document ideas, notes, experiments, and projects related to:**
- World Models
- Agent Systems
- Self-Awareness
- Digital Minds

My goal is to better understand both human intelligence and the future of artificial intelligence.
